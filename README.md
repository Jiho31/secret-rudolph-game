## Instructions
 - Create a customised game and share the link to let others play your game.
 - In the game, rudolph will move left/right to collect gifts to gain or lose points.
 - Check the score board and see how well others played, and find out what your friend wants for Christmas!ğŸ„ğŸŒŸ

## How to create your game
1. Select 1-3 items you would like and dislike
2. Confirm your selections and create the game
3. Share your game link to your families/friends
4. Watch them play! (Save your code to check the score board later)

## How to play
- Move Rudolph to collect the dropping items that you think your friend likes, and avoid the ones they would dislike!
